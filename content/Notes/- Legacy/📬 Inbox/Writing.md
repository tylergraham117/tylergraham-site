# Writing


### Cliche Ideas
Cliche ideas lead to bored audiences. They involve ideas that are so simple and common that there is no real argument against what you are presenting, and the writing is of no surpise to anyone. The concept could be too broad or too obvious.

[Avoid Cliche Ideas](https://perell.com/note/avoid-cliche-ideas/)

Malcom Gladwell mentions something similar in his writing course. He talks about how you should attempt to find imperfect ideas, because at least they are interesting.
